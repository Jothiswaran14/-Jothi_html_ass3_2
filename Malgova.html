11. Malgova, Tamilnadu

Fresh Salem Malgova a favourite fruit for all south Indians loves to taste at least a piece. Malgova tastes delectably sweet, a mango that tastes like milk khoa. Fruit is large in size, roundish oblique in shape and yellow in colour having thick outer skin and hard seed inside. Salem is very famous for Malgova mangoes, a native variety of Tamil Nadu.
